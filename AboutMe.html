About Me

I am a Junior Software Developer with two years of professional experience, primarily focused on VBScript. I began my journey in software development learning VBScript on the job, where I developed and refined my abilities in building and maintaining software solutions using this language.
While I’ve gained a strong understanding of VBScript, I recognize that it is an older, outdated language with limitations in terms of scalability, maintainability, and modern software practices. To address these challenges and help the company evolve its software development capabilities, I have embarked on an Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) course. This course not only aligns with my company’s goals of modernizing its software but also supports my personal development as a developer.
I am excited to continue growing as a developer and contributing to impactful projects that make a difference.
